Our Story

Empowering students
to achieve their dreams.

BilimUp! ("Rise with Knowledge" in Uzbek) democratizes quality test prep. Founded in 2026 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, we're on a mission to help 1 million students study abroad by 2030.

What We Stand For
🎯
Mission
BilimUp! democratizes quality test prep. Founded in 2026 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan — we combine world-class content with local understanding.
👁️
Vision
Help 1 million students achieve study abroad dreams by 2030 through accessible, engaging learning tools available in multiple languages.
💡
Approach
Cognitive science meets test strategies. Created by former IELTS examiners and SAT perfect scorers who understand what it takes.
